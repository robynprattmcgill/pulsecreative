The Process

Simple by design.
Powerful by nature.

No banks. No gatekeepers. No confusing terms. The Pot is a rotating savings circle that turns community into capital — automatically.

  1. 01
    Create or Join a Pot Start your own savings circle or accept an invite. You set the amount, the cadence, the crew.
  2. 02
    Everyone Contributes Each member drops in their share every cycle. The app handles reminders, tracking, and receipts.
  3. 03
    One Person Gets the Pot Rotating payouts go to a different member each cycle until everyone has received their full sum.
  4. 04
    Build & Repeat Track your savings history, grow your credit story, and run multiple pots simultaneously.
